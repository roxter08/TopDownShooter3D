The zipped projects contains a "Build" folder where you can find the android .apk build for this game.

The Player controls for this game are as follows:

For Mobile: 
	Move (On screen joystick) 
	Shoot (Shoot Button)
	Swap Weapon( Swap Button) 

For Editor: 
	Move (Arrow / WASD Keys) 
	Shoot (Spacebar)
	Swap Weapon('C' Key)

Enemies are of 2 types as mentioned in the instruction: 

	Yellow headed enemies move randomly on the map
	Red headed enemies will move towards the player when in the chasing range and stops and shoot when the player is in it's shooting range
